Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

No Data Collection

Salary Negotiation Script Builder does not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter. All script generation happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your CTC figures, role, city, and industry details never leave your device.

No Server

This tool has no backend. There are no databases, no logs, and no analytics that track what you type. Your financial information stays private.

This tool does not use cookies or local storage. Nothing is saved on your device between sessions.
